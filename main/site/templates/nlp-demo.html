Demonstration

The generated similarity score between two questions is a weighted sum of two comparison algorithms: a vectorized TF-IDF dot product, and a fuzzy string match.

The equation for the weighted similarity score is as follows: \[ S(t_1, t_2) = tf\_idf(t_1, t_2)(\alpha) + fuzzy(t_1, t_2)(1-\alpha)\] Titles and bodies for posts are also weighted differently. The outputs of each of the functions above are actually themselves weighted sums, where the title similarity score has weight \(\beta\) and the body \((1-\beta)\)
